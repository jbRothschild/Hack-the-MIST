      How can merchants reduce the carbon footprint from shipping Shopify?
      You can reduce or remove the carbon dioxide emissions from packages that you ship in multiple ways:
      1. by using compostable packaging.
      2. by using carbon-neutral shipping options.
      3. by supporting initiatives that contribute to carbon offset or removal.
